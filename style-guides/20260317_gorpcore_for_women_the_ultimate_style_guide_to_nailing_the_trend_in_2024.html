Gorpcore for Women:
Your 2024 Style Guide

Outfit Ideas · How to Wear It · Shop the Essentials

Okay bestie, let's talk about the trend that's absolutely eating up your FYP right now — gorpcore for women. What started as "granola girl meets high fashion" has become a full-blown aesthetic movement. Think Arc'teryx jackets styled with ballet flats, Salomon sneakers with mini skirts, and puffer vests over literally everything. It's giving outdoorsy, it's giving effortless, and honestly? It's giving main character energy on every trail and every brunch. Whether you saw it on TikTok or your IG Explore page, here's your no-BS style guide on how to wear gorpcore without looking like you're about to summit Everest.

1. Start With One Statement Outdoor Piece

The secret to gorpcore outfit ideas that actually slay? Don't go full REI. Pick one technical piece — a shell jacket, a fleece vest, or trail sneakers — and style the rest feminine and minimal. A cropped Gore-Tex jacket over a slip dress? Obsessed. It's all about that contrast, babe.

2. Lean Into Earth Tones (But Make It Chic)

Olive, rust, cream, slate, mushroom brown — this palette is the backbone of gorpcore for women. Layer different shades of the same tone family for that quiet-luxury-meets-wilderness vibe. Pro tip: a monochromatic earth-tone fit photographs insanely well for the 'gram. You're welcome.

3. Accessories Are Doing the Heavy Lifting

Carabiner keychains clipped to your bag, a nylon crossbody, a beanie that costs more than your rent — gorpcore accessories are the easiest entry point. A simple paracord bracelet or a technical belt over baggy cargos can take a basic outfit from "meh" to "she gets it" real quick.

4. Mix High & Low Like a Pro

The girlies who do gorpcore best know how to wear a $30 fleece with tailored trousers and make it look editorial. Pair budget-friendly trail runners with your nicest wool coat. Style a utility vest over a cashmere knit. The juxtaposition is literally the whole point — and it makes every outfit feel intentional.
