Eclectic Grandpa Style Is Taking Over My FYP — Here's How to Nail It

Trend Guide · Updated 2025

Okay besties, if your TikTok and Instagram feeds haven't been absolutely flooded with oversized knit vests, corduroy trousers, and librarian-core vibes — where have you been? Eclectic grandpa style is THE aesthetic right now, and honestly? It's giving cozy, sophisticated, and effortlessly cool all at once. Think: your grandfather's closet meets a vintage thrift haul meets main character energy.

This trend went viral because it's the ultimate anti-trend trend — it's not about being polished or matchy-matchy. It's about layering textures, mixing patterns, and looking like you raided the most iconic wardrobe ever. Whether you're already obsessed or just starting to vibe with it, this style guide has everything you need. Let's get into these outfit ideas. 🤎

01

Layer Like Your Life Depends On It

The core of eclectic grandpa style is layering — and lots of it. Start with a classic button-down (oversized, obviously), throw a chunky knit sweater vest on top, and finish with a tweed or wool blazer. The key is mixing textures: think cable knit over crisp cotton under herringbone. It looks intentionally undone and that's the whole point. No gatekeeping here — more layers = more slay.

02

Embrace the Earth Tones (With a Twist)

Your color palette is warm and muted — browns, tans, olive, burgundy, mustard, and cream. But here's how to wear it without looking like a literal pile of leaves: add one unexpected pop. A pair of vintage-looking plaid trousers, a quirky brooch, or mismatched argyle socks. That "eclectic" part matters. You're not just grandpa — you're the cool grandpa who collects rare vinyl and has opinions about architecture.

03

Accessories Are Non-Negotiable

This is where the look goes from "oh cute" to "literally obsessed." Round wire-frame glasses (even non-prescription), a leather watch with a worn strap, a newsboy cap, or a structured leather bag — these details are what make the aesthetic *chef's kiss*. Also lowkey a great time to invest in a good loafer. Penny loafers are having their MOMENT and your feet deserve that energy.

04

Fit Matters More Than You Think

Hot take: eclectic grandpa style only works when the proportions are right. Oversized on top? Balance with a slightly tapered or cropped trouser. Wide-leg pants? Tuck in your shirt and cinch with a braided leather belt. You want "borrowed from grandpa" not "lost in grandpa's clothes." Proportion play is what separates a good outfit idea from a great one.

Eclectic grandpa style is proof that fashion doesn't have to be complicated to be iconic. Raid a thrift store, raid your actual grandpa's closet (with permission, obvi), and just have fun with it. The beauty of this trend is that it's inherently yours — no two fits will ever look the same. Now go serve. 🫶
