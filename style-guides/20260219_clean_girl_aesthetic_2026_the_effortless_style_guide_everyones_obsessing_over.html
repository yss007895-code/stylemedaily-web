How to Nail the Clean Girl Look: 4 Essential Style Tips

1. Master the Minimal Color Palette

Stick to neutrals like cream, beige, white, and soft browns. This how to wear clean girl tip is non-negotiable – monochromatic looks are your best friend. Think tonal dressing but make it chic.

2. Embrace Oversized Silhouettes

Baggy jeans, oversized blazers, and loose-fitting knits are staples. The key is looking put-together while feeling like you're wearing pajamas. It's giving comfort but make it fashion.

3. Gold Jewelry Only

Silver who? Clean girl aesthetic 2026 is all about warm gold tones. Stack delicate chains, add small hoops, and keep it minimal but impactful.

4. Perfect the Undone-Done Hair

Slicked-back buns, glass-like straight hair, or that "I didn't try" wave. Use a good hair oil and call it a day. The messier, the better – but in a controlled way, obviously.
