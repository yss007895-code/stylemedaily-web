Your Coastal Grandmother Style Guide

Obsessed with the look but not sure where to start? Don't sweat it. Here are a few core outfit ideas and tips on how to wear the trend without looking dated.

1. Live in Linen

Seriously, linen is your new best friend. An oversized white or blue linen button-down is non-negotiable. Wear it over a simple tank, with matching linen pants, or as a beach cover-up. The key is to embrace the relaxed, slightly rumpled texture. It's a low-key flex that screams "I just got back from my garden."

2. Master the Neutral Palette

The entire coastal grandmother style is built on a foundation of neutrals. We're talking white, cream, beige, khaki, and soft blues. A monochromatic look (like cream trousers with a matching knit sweater) is peak coastal grandma. It's clean, sophisticated, and makes getting dressed a breeze.

3. Accessorize with Intention

This is where you seal the deal. Think simple, timeless pieces. A woven straw or canvas tote bag is a must for your farmers' market runs. Add a pair of classic sunglasses, delicate gold jewelry, and a packable sun hat. The vibe is curated, not cluttered.
