Eclectic Grandpa Style Is the Main Character Energy We All Need

Your style guide to the coziest, most unhinged aesthetic on TikTok right now

Okay bestie, let's talk about the aesthetic that's absolutely dominating every FYP and Explore page right now: eclectic grandpa style. Think oversized knits, mismatched patterns, corduroy everything, and the energy of a retired professor who summers in Tuscany. It's giving cozy. It's giving intentional. It's giving "I raided my grandpa's closet and somehow look like a Vogue editorial." And honestly? We're obsessed.

Whether you've been pinning these outfit ideas for months or you're just now discovering this vibe, here's your no-gatekeeping style guide on how to wear eclectic grandpa like a total pro.

1. The Oversized Blazer Is Your Best Friend

This is non-negotiable. A boxy, slightly slouchy blazer in brown, camel, or forest green is the backbone of every eclectic grandpa outfit. Layer it over a simple turtleneck or even a graphic tee for that effortless "I didn't try but I ate" energy. Bonus points if it's tweed or has elbow patches — the more "university lecture hall circa 1978," the better.

2. Mix Patterns Like You Mean It

Here's where the "eclectic" part really comes in. Argyle sweater vest + plaid trousers? Yes. Houndstooth coat over a striped button-down? Absolutely unhinged and absolutely correct. The key to making pattern-mixing work: keep your color palette in the same warm, muted family — think browns, olives, creams, and burgundy. It looks curated, not chaotic.

3. Invest in "Old Man" Accessories

The accessories are what take this from "cute outfit" to "I have a personality." We're talking round wire-frame glasses, leather watchbands, newsboy caps, and a crossbody satchel that looks like it holds first-edition novels. A chunky knit scarf draped (not tied — draped, very important) over your shoulders is the chef's kiss finishing touch.

4. Footwear: Loafers or Bust

Sneakers are cool, but penny loafers and chunky Oxford shoes are the slay move here. Brown leather or suede keeps things authentic to the grandpa aesthetic. For a more casual day, clean white New Balance 550s also low-key work — because even grandpa has his off-duty moments.

The beauty of eclectic grandpa style is that it's all about self-expression with zero pretension. It's cozy, it's chic, and it literally works for every body type because oversized silhouettes are inherently inclusive. So raid the thrift stores, screenshot those outfit ideas, and let your inner distinguished gentleman out. You're not just following a trend — you're building a wardrobe with actual character. And that? That will never go out of style. 🤎
