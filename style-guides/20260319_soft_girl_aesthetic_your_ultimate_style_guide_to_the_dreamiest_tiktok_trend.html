Soft Girl Aesthetic:
The TikTok Trend That's Taking Over

5 min read · Style Guide · Updated 2025

Okay bestie, let's talk about the soft girl aesthetic — because your FYP is absolutely flooded with it and honestly? We're not mad. Think pastel everything, delicate gold jewelry, plush textures, and that effortlessly dreamy vibe that makes you look like you just stepped out of a Pinterest board. This trend has been going viral on TikTok and Instagram for good reason: it's feminine, cozy, and genuinely wearable. Whether you're already a soft girl at heart or just looking for fresh outfit ideas, this style guide is your sign to lean ALL the way in. 🌸

1. Pastels Are Your New Neutrals

The core of the soft girl aesthetic is a dreamy pastel palette — lavender, baby pink, powder blue, and sage green. The key to making it look chic (and not costume-y) is tonal dressing. Pair a lilac knit cardigan with cream wide-leg trousers, or go full blush monochrome. How to wear pastels without looking like an Easter egg? Stick to two to three shades max per outfit and ground the look with white or beige basics. No notes, just vibes.

2. Layer With Cozy, Feminine Textures

Soft girl energy is literally in the name — so we're talking fuzzy cardigans, angora sweaters, satin midi skirts, and knit vests. Layering is *chef's kiss* for this aesthetic. Throw an oversized cable-knit over a flowy floral dress and you've got one of those outfit ideas that looks like it took zero effort but actually eats. Pro tip: mixing textures (like ribbed knit + silk) adds dimension and keeps things elevated. Very quiet luxury meets soft girl coded.

3. Accessorize Dainty, Not Heavy

The accessories make or break this look fr. We're reaching for thin gold chain necklaces, pearl studs, claw clips in pastel tortoiseshell, and mini crossbody bags in blush or cream. Hair bows and ribbon headbands? Absolutely yes — they're the soft girl's signature. Skip anything chunky or statement-heavy. The goal is delicate and intentional. Stack a few dainty rings, add a silk scrunchie, and you're giving main character energy without even trying.

4. Footwear: Keep It Clean & Classic

For shoes, think ballet flats (the It Girl shoe that refuses to leave), white platform sneakers, or strappy kitten heels in nude. Basically anything that feels feminine and polished without competing with the rest of your outfit. If you want to know how to wear the soft girl aesthetic year-round, invest in versatile footwear that transitions from brunch to campus to date night. Trust — ballet flats with a midi skirt and cardigan combo is the outfit idea that keeps on giving.
