Coastal Grandmother Style: Your Ultimate Guide to the Aesthetic That Broke TikTok

STYLE GUIDE  ·  5 MIN READ

Okay bestie, let's talk about the coastal grandmother style — the aesthetic that literally took over everyone's FYP and IG Explore page. Think Nancy Meyers movies, Diane Keaton in Something's Gotta Give, linen everything, and main-character energy at a Hamptons farmer's market. It's giving elegance, it's giving ease, and honestly? It's giving I have my life together even when I absolutely do not. 🐚

Whether you live nowhere near a coast or you're fully landlocked in Ohio — doesn't matter. This vibe is a mood, not a zip code. Here's your no-fluff style guide on how to wear coastal grandmother like a pro.

1. Linen Is Your Entire Personality Now

No literally. Wide-leg linen pants, oversized linen button-downs, linen blazers — this is the foundation of every coastal grandmother outfit idea. Stick to a neutral palette: oatmeal, cream, soft white, light khaki, and powder blue. The more effortlessly rumpled, the better. Ironing? We don't know her.

2. Layer Like You're Going on a Sunset Walk (Even If You're Not)

The key to nailing this aesthetic is thoughtful layering. A cotton cable-knit sweater draped over your shoulders? Chef's kiss. A lightweight cashmere cardigan over a white tank and flowing trousers? Immaculate vibes. The trick: keep everything relaxed and slightly oversized. Nothing tight, nothing fussy. You're basically cosplaying as someone who owns beachfront property and reads literary fiction for fun.

3. Accessories: Less Is Literally More

Ditch the chunky jewelry and go minimal. A simple gold pendant, small hoop earrings, and a classic woven tote bag are your holy trinity. Sunglasses are non-negotiable — go for oversized tortoiseshell or classic aviators. And a straw hat? That's not optional, that's required. This is a how to wear accessories masterclass: one or two pieces that look expensive, even if they're from Amazon. 💅

4. Footwear: Keep It Grounded

Leather sandals, white canvas sneakers, or simple loafers — that's the whole rotation. No platforms, no heels, no chaos. You're walking to a seaside bookshop, not the club. Birkenstock-style sandals are peak coastal grandma and honestly they're so comfy it's unhinged. Your feet will thank you and your outfit ideas will always look polished.
