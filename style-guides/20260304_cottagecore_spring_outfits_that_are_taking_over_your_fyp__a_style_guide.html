Cottagecore Spring Outfits That Are Taking Over Your FYP

STYLE GUIDE • SPRING 2025 • 4 MIN READ

Okay bestie, let's talk about the aesthetic that absolutely refuses to leave our feeds — cottagecore spring outfits. If your TikTok FYP is flooded with flowy midi skirts, puff sleeves, and "picking wildflowers in the English countryside" energy, you're not alone. This trend has been simmering for a few years, but in 2025? It's fully main character coded. Think soft, romantic, a little whimsical — but make it wearable for your actual life (brunch, farmer's market, that picnic date you're manifesting).

Whether you're a cottagecore veteran or just here for the outfit ideas, this style guide breaks down exactly how to wear the look without feeling like you're in a costume. Let's get into it. 🌿

01

Lead With Linen — Always

Linen is literally the backbone of every cottagecore spring outfit. A relaxed linen midi dress in cream, sage, or butter yellow is the easiest one-and-done look. Pro tip: go slightly oversized and cinch it with a woven belt for that effortlessly undone silhouette. It's giving "I woke up in Provence" and honestly? That's the vibe we're chasing. Linen shorts and a breezy blouse work too — just keep the palette soft and earthy.

02

Puff Sleeves + Ditsy Florals = Serotonin

No cottagecore style guide is complete without mentioning puff sleeves. They're dramatic but in the most delicate way. Pair a ditsy floral puff-sleeve top with high-waisted wide-leg trousers for a look that's polished enough for real life but still screams "I forage for herbs in my garden." The key here: keep prints small-scale and muted. We're not doing Hawaiian shirt florals — think vintage wallpaper, dried botanical vibes. This is one of the easiest outfit ideas to recreate from your existing closet too.

03

Layer Like a Storybook Character

Spring weather is chaotic (we know), so layering is non-negotiable. The cottagecore move? A knit cardigan draped over your shoulders or a lightweight lace-trim camisole peeking out from under a chunky open-knit sweater. Bonus points for a vest — yes, a vest — in cable knit or quilted fabric. It's the cozy-meets-cute layering piece that's been blowing up on IG Reels. Throw it over a white button-down with a long skirt and you're literally cottagecore perfection.

04

Accessories Are the Final Boss

The details take your cottagecore spring outfits from "cute" to "she has a Pinterest board dedicated to this." Think: ribbon hair bows, straw basket bags, dainty gold jewelry, and Mary Jane flats or leather ankle boots. A woven sun hat instantly elevates even the most basic outfit. And here's the cheat code — a single fresh flower tucked behind your ear or clipped into your hair. It costs nothing and it's unbelievably photogenic. Your IG engagement will thank you.

And that's the whole vibe, babe. Cottagecore spring outfits are really just about embracing softness, natural textures, and that romanticized slow-living energy — but styled in a way that feels modern and you. Mix these pieces with stuff you already own, don't stress about being "perfect," and remember: the best outfit ideas are the ones that make you feel like the main character of your own little story.

Now go touch some grass (aesthetically). 🌾✨
