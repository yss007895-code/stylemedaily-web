It's Giving Grandpacore: Your Guide to the Eclectic Grandpa Aesthetic

If your TikTok and IG feeds are suddenly flooded with chunky knits, tailored trousers, and vintage-y loafers, you're not imagining things. The 'eclectic grandpa' trend is the viral aesthetic of the season, and honestly, we're so here for it. It's all about raiding a hypothetical (and very stylish) grandpa's closetâ€”mixing textures, classic silhouettes, and a touch of quirky personality. This style guide breaks down how to wear the trend without looking like you're in costume.

Key Outfit Ideas for the Vibe

1. Master the Vest Game. The sweater vest is the heart of eclectic grandpa style. Don't just throw it over a tee (though that's a start). Layer a slightly oversized, argyle or cable-knit vest over a crisp, relaxed-fit button-down shirt. Let the cuffs and collar pop out. It's preppy, but make it fashion. This is one of the easiest ways to nail the look.

2. It's All About the Trousers. Ditch the skinny jeans. This aesthetic calls for comfortable, structured pants. Think high-waisted, pleated trousers in neutral colors like beige, brown, or grey. A slightly wide-leg or straight-leg cut adds to the relaxed, grandpa-on-his-day-off vibe. Bonus points if they're corduroy or wool.

3. Lean into Loafers. The perfect footwear for this trend is a classic loafer. Whether it's a chunky lug-sole or a sleek penny loafer, this shoe grounds the entire outfit. The real pro move? Pair them with visible, slightly scrunched-down crew socks in a fun color or a classic white. This small detail is what elevates the entire outfit from standard to a certified style moment.
