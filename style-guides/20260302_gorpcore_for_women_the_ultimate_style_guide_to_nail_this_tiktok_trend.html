Gorpcore for Women:
Your Chic Style Guide

TREND REPORT · OUTFIT IDEAS · SPRING 2025

🏔️🧥✨

Okay bestie, let's talk about the trend that has absolutely taken over your FYP and Explore page: gorpcore for women. What started as outdoorsy guys wearing fleece vests to brunch has evolved into one of the most wearable, cool-girl aesthetics of the year. Think functional meets fashion — hiking boots with mini skirts, puffer vests layered over slip dresses, and cargo pants that actually hit. It's giving "I could summit a mountain but I chose to get oat milk lattes instead," and honestly? We're obsessed.

Whether you've been gorpcore-curious or you're ready to go all in, this style guide breaks down exactly how to wear the trend without looking like you raided your dad's REI closet. These outfit ideas are cute, versatile, and extremely screenshot-worthy. Let's get into it. 👇

Tip 01

Balance Utility with Femininity

The secret sauce of gorpcore for women is contrast. Pair a boxy fleece or oversized windbreaker with something more fitted and feminine underneath — a ribbed tank, a bodysuit, or even a satin cami. Cargo pants? Size up and belt them at the waist. The vibe is "effortlessly cool" not "I'm literally going camping." Pro tip: earth tones (olive, rust, cream, slate) are your best friends here. No notes.

Tip 02

Invest in Statement Outerwear

Your jacket IS the outfit. A cropped puffer vest, a retro-colored fleece zip-up, or a technical shell jacket in an unexpected color (sage green, butter yellow, lavender) will carry every single look. Layer it over literally anything — a hoodie, a knit dress, a basic tee — and suddenly you have outfit ideas for days. This one piece does the heavy lifting so you don't have to. Slay and stay warm? Iconic behavior.

Tip 03

Shoes Make or Break the Look

This is non-negotiable. The footwear is what takes gorpcore from "random" to "she knows exactly what she's doing." Chunky trail sneakers, hiking-style boots, or even elevated Birkenstock-adjacent sandals are the move. For a more polished how to wear approach, pair bulky outdoor shoes with straight-leg or wide-leg pants that have a slight break. For weekends? Trail runners with bike shorts and a fleece. Unmatched energy.

Tip 04

Accessorize Like You Mean It

The little details are what separate "I just threw this on" from a curated gorpcore moment. Think: a carabiner keychain clipped to your bag, a sporty crossbody or sling bag, a simple baseball cap in a muted tone, or layered necklaces peeking out of a half-zip. Functional accessories that double as style statements? That's the whole thesis of this style guide. Keep it minimal but intentional.
