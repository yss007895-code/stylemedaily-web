The Soft Girl Aesthetic Is Still Running TikTok — Here's How to Wear It

5 min read · Updated June 2025

Okay bestie, let's talk about the soft girl aesthetic — because it's not just a phase, it's a whole lifestyle at this point. From pastel cardigans to dainty gold jewelry flooding your FYP, this trend has been dominating TikTok and Instagram for good reason: it's feminine, effortless, and genuinely wearable IRL. Whether you're looking for fresh outfit ideas for brunch or just want to romanticize your daily fits, consider this your official style guide. No gatekeeping here. 🌸

1. Start With a Pastel Base (It's Non-Negotiable)

The foundation of every soft girl outfit? Pastels on pastels. Think lavender knit tops, baby pink pleated skirts, and powder blue cardigans. The key to how to wear this without looking costume-y is to stick to a tonal palette — layer shades within the same color family for that Pinterest-worthy monochromatic moment. A blush satin cami under a cream oversized blazer? *Chef's kiss.*

2. Accessories Are Doing the Heavy Lifting

This is where the aesthetic really clicks. Dainty gold necklaces, pearl hair clips, ribbon bows, and mini heart-shaped bags are your best friends. Layer your jewelry — stack thin rings, double up on necklaces at different lengths. The vibe is "I woke up this ethereal" even though we all know you spent 20 minutes coordinating. And honestly? Worth it.

3. Embrace the Soft Textures

Fabrics matter SO much with this look. Reach for angora, chenille knits, satin, tulle, and fuzzy cardigans. If it looks like a cloud, you're doing it right. A chunky knit cardigan over a flowy floral dress is literally the soft girl uniform — and it works for every season. Pro tip: swap sneakers for ballet flats or Mary Janes to complete the soft girl aesthetic properly.

4. Keep Your Makeup & Nails on Theme

Your fit doesn't stop at clothes, babe. Glazed donut nails, rosy blush placed high on the cheeks, and a soft pink lip gloss tie the whole look together. The goal is "healthy, glowy, I-drink-water" energy. Keep brows fluffy and natural, and if you're feeling bold, add a tiny heart stamp under your eye. It's giving main character.
