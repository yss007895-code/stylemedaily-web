Indie Sleaze Is Back & It's Giving Main Character Energy

TREND GUIDE  ·  6 MIN READ

Okay, let's talk about the trend that has absolutely taken over your FYP and explore page — the indie sleaze revival. Think 2007-2012 Tumblr energy: smudged eyeliner, American Apparel vibes, leather jackets thrown over everything, and an attitude that screams "I woke up like this but also curated every detail." If you've been seeing fishnets, band tees, and messy hair all over TikTok, congratulations — you're already in the loop. Now let's talk outfit ideas and how to wear this look without it feeling like a costume.

The beauty of indie sleaze is that it's inherently anti-perfectionist. It's the polar opposite of the clean girl aesthetic — and honestly? We needed the chaos. This style guide is your roadmap to nailing it.

1. The Foundation: A Beat-Up Leather Jacket

No indie sleaze outfit idea is complete without one. Go for a slightly oversized, distressed moto jacket — not pristine, not try-hard. Layer it over literally anything: a slip dress, a cropped band tee, a bodycon mini. The jacket IS the outfit. Bonus points if it looks like it survived a warehouse party. Thrift stores are your best friend here, but a good faux-leather option works just as well.

2. Skirts Over Jeans? No. Tights Under Everything? Yes.

Sheer black tights or fishnets are the unsung hero of this entire aesthetic. Pair them under denim shorts, mini skirts, or even ripped-up dresses. They add that perfectly undone, "going out on a Tuesday" energy. When figuring out how to wear fishnets in 2024, the key is to keep the rest of the outfit relatively simple — a basic tank, a micro mini, and chunky boots. Let the texture do the talking.

3. Band Tees, But Make It Fashion

This is non-negotiable. Grab a vintage (or vintage-looking) band tee — The Strokes, Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Arctic Monkeys, whatever speaks to your soul — and style it tucked into a high-waisted leather skirt or knotted at the waist over low-rise jeans. Yes, low-rise is part of this indie sleaze revival conversation and no, I will not be taking questions. If you're not ready for full Y2K hip-bone exposure, mid-rise works too. We're inclusive here.

4. Accessories: Messy, Layered, Intentional Chaos

Pile on the silver jewelry — chunky rings, layered chain necklaces, maybe a studded belt slung low on your hips. Sunglasses should be oversized or tiny wayfarers (no in-between). A crossbody bag that's small and slightly beat up completes the look. And please, for the love of indie sleaze, your hair should look like you just left a concert. Bedhead is the vibe. Undone waves, zero effort. Perfection is the enemy here.
