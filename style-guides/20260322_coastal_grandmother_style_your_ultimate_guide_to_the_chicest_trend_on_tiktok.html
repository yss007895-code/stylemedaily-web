Coastal Grandmother Style: Your Ultimate Guide to the Chicest Trend on TikTok

STYLE GUIDE  ·  5 MIN READ

Okay bestie, let's talk about the aesthetic that has absolutely taken over our FYPs: coastal grandmother style. Think Nancy Meyers heroines — Diane Keaton in Something's Gotta Give, Meryl Streep sipping wine overlooking the ocean. It's linen, it's neutral, it's effortlessly chic, and honestly? It's giving main character energy without even trying. This style guide is your go-to for nailing the look whether you're 22 or 35.

The reason this trend went viral isn't just the vibe — it's the accessibility. You don't need a Hamptons beach house. You just need the right outfit ideas and a few wardrobe staples. Let me break it down. 👇

1. Linen Is Your Literal Best Friend

No coastal grandmother outfit ideas list is complete without linen. Wide-leg linen trousers, an oversized linen button-down, a breezy linen midi dress — literally any of these will do. The key? Keep it relaxed, slightly oversized, and in soft neutrals like oat, cream, soft blue, or khaki. Wrinkles are not only acceptable, they're the whole point. Unbothered queen behavior only.

2. Layer Like You Own a Cottage by the Sea

Knowing how to wear layers casually is peak coastal grandma. Drape a cotton cable-knit sweater over your shoulders (yes, like a cape — trust the process). Pair a lightweight turtleneck under a structured blazer. A cozy cardigan over a white tee and wide-leg pants? *Chef's kiss.* The layering is what takes it from basic to Nancy-Meyers-cinematic-universe.

3. Accessories: Keep It Quiet Luxury

We're talking woven tote bags, delicate gold jewelry, tortoiseshell sunglasses, and a classic wide-brim sun hat. No logos screaming at you. This style guide is all about understated elegance — the kind that whispers "I summer in Nantucket" even if you've never left Ohio. A silk scarf tied loosely around your neck or on your bag handle? Immaculate vibes.

4. Footwear: Comfort Meets Chic

Ditch the heels, babe. Coastal grandmother style is all about leather sandals, white canvas sneakers, or classic loafers. Think shoes you could actually walk on a boardwalk in without suffering. Birkenstock-style slides and espadrilles also absolutely eat here. Comfort IS the flex.

So whether you're building outfit ideas for a beach vacation or just want to look effortlessly put-together at brunch, this is your sign to embrace it. 🤍
