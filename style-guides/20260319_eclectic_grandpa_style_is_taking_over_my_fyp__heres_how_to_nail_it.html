Eclectic Grandpa Style Is Taking Over My FYP — Here's How to Nail It

Trend Guide · Updated 2025

Okay bestie, let's talk about the eclectic grandpa style trend that's absolutely everywhere on TikTok and Instagram right now. You know the vibe — oversized knit vests, pleated trousers, loafers that look like they were borrowed from your grandpa's closet (in the best way). It's giving cozy intellectual, it's giving "I read poetry at a café in Vermont," and honestly? It's the most effortlessly cool aesthetic of 2025. This style guide is your cheat code to making it work without looking like you're wearing a costume. Let's get into these outfit ideas. 🤎

Styling Tips

1. The Oversized Knit Vest Is Your BFF

This is literally the cornerstone piece when figuring out how to wear eclectic grandpa style. Layer a chunky argyle or cable-knit sweater vest over a crisp button-down (bonus points if it's slightly oversized). Tuck just the front of the shirt for that effortless "I woke up like this" energy. The key? Let everything be a little slouchy — no snatched fits here.

2. Pleated Trousers > Everything

Skinny jeans are so out it's not even funny. For these outfit ideas, you want wide-leg or straight-leg pleated trousers in earth tones — think khaki, chocolate brown, olive, or oatmeal. Corduroy? Absolutely unhinged in the best way. Pair them slightly cropped to show off your loafers or chunky socks. This silhouette is *the* move.

3. Accessories That Scream "Distinguished Gentleman"

This is where the "eclectic" part comes alive. A leather-band watch, round tortoiseshell glasses (even non-prescription — no shame), a tweed newsboy cap, or a vintage-looking crossbody bag. Mix textures and eras. The whole point of this eclectic grandpa style is that it looks collected over decades, not bought in one shopping haul. Layering a wool scarf loosely is also very main character behavior.

4. Color Palette: Keep It Warm & Earthy

Your style guide color cheat sheet: rust, forest green, cream, camel, burgundy, and warm grays. Avoid anything neon or super saturated. Think autumn library, not summer music festival. The magic is in mixing patterns within this palette — plaid blazer + striped shirt? That's a slay. Just keep the tones in the same warm family and it all looks intentional.

Shop the Essentials

Build your eclectic grandpa wardrobe with these key pieces:

☐ Oversized Argyle Knit Sweater Vest

Relaxed fit, neutral tones — layer over button-downs or turtlenecks

☐ Wide-Leg Pleated Corduroy Trousers

High-waisted, cropped ankle — in brown, olive, or cream

☐ Classic Oxford Button-Down Shirt

Slightly oversized, light blue or white stripe — the foundation piece

☐ Chunky Penny Loafers

Cognac or black leather with lug soles for a modern twist

☐ Round Tortoiseshell Glasses (Blue Light or Clear Lens)

Instant grandpa-chic vibes — functional and fashionable

☐ Oversized Plaid Wool Blazer

Thrifted energy even if it's brand new — earth-tone plaid is chef's kiss

☐ Vintage-Style Leather Crossbody Bag

Small, structured, with brass hardware — very "retired professor"

The eclectic grandpa style trend proves that fashion doesn't have to be loud to make a statement. It's cozy, it's timeless, and it's giving main character on a budget. Now go raid your grandpa's closet (with permission, obviously). ✨
