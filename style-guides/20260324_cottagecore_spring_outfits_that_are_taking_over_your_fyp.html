Cottagecore Spring Outfits That Are Taking Over Your FYP

Spring 2025 · Style Guide · Outfit Ideas

Okay bestie, let's talk about the aesthetic that literally refuses to leave our feeds — cottagecore spring outfits are having their main character moment again, and honestly? We're not mad about it. From TikTok's "get ready with me" videos to Instagram's curated flatlays, this dreamy, romantic vibe is everywhere. Think wildflower fields, linen everything, and that effortless "I woke up in a French countryside" energy. Whether you're a cottagecore girlie at heart or just trend-curious, this style guide is your sign to lean ALL the way in. 🌿

1. The Flowy Midi Dress Is Non-Negotiable

If you're wondering how to wear cottagecore without looking like you're in costume, start here. A floral or ditsy-print midi dress in muted tones — think sage, cream, dusty rose — is the backbone of this entire aesthetic. The silhouette should be relaxed, slightly cinched at the waist, and absolutely giving "picnic date." Pair it with woven flats or ballet shoes and you're literally done. Low effort, high slay.

2. Layer With a Knit Cardigan (Trust)

Spring weather is unhinged — it's 70° one minute and freezing the next. A chunky or open-knit cardigan in oatmeal, butter yellow, or lavender is the move. Drape it over your shoulders for that editorial cottagecore moment, or button it up as a top with a long skirt. This one layering piece turns basic outfit ideas into something that looks extremely curated. The girlies on TikTok have been doing this all season and it hits every time.

3. Linen Pants + Puff Sleeve Top = Chef's Kiss

Not a dress person? No worries — cottagecore isn't gatekept by dresses. Wide-leg linen pants in white or beige paired with a puff-sleeve blouse is equally iconic. Tuck in the top, add a woven belt, and you've got an outfit that works for brunch, farmers markets, or that aesthetic coffee shop you've been meaning to visit. This combo is lowkey one of the best cottagecore spring outfits for people who want comfort without sacrificing the vibe.

4. Accessories Are Doing the Heavy Lifting

The details are what separate a cute outfit from a *moment*. Straw bags, ribbon hair bows, dainty gold jewelry, and lace-trim socks are the finishing touches that make your look scream cottagecore. Pro tip on how to wear these without going overboard: pick two accent accessories max. A straw crossbody + ribbon bow? Perfection. Pearl earrings + lace socks with loafers? Absolutely elite. Keep it intentional and it'll look elevated, not costume-y.
