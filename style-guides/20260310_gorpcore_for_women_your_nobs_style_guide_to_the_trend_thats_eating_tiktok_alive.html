Gorpcore for Women: Your No-BS Style Guide to the Trend That's Eating TikTok Alive

Outfit Ideas · How to Wear · Spring 2025

Okay bestie, let's talk about the aesthetic that's been absolutely dominating your FYP: gorpcore for women. What started as outdoorsy dads wearing fleece vests has evolved into one of the chicest, most wearable trends of 2025. Think trail-meets-city — hiking boots with mini skirts, puffer vests over slip dresses, carabiners as accessories. It's giving main character energy on a mountain, and honestly? It's never looked better.

The reason gorpcore is so viral is simple: it's comfortable, functional, AND fashion-forward. TikTok creators and Instagram It-girls have made "Good Ol' Raisins and Peanuts" (yes, that's what GORP stands for 😭) look like it belongs on a runway. If you've been wondering how to wear this trend without looking like you're literally about to summit Everest, I got you.

4 Styling Tips to Nail Gorpcore (Without Trying Too Hard)

1. Contrast Is Everything 🏔️✨

The secret sauce to gorpcore outfit ideas that actually slay? Mix rugged with refined. Pair a chunky hiking boot with tailored cargo trousers. Layer a North Face-style puffer over a fitted turtleneck and gold hoops. The juxtaposition is what makes it fashion and not just… camping.

2. Earth Tones Are Your Best Friend 🤎

Olive, rust, cream, slate, mushroom — these are your go-to colors. A monochromatic earth-tone fit instantly reads elevated gorpcore. Pro tip: add one pop of color (a bright orange beanie or moss green crossbody) to keep it fresh and very much giving Pinterest mood board.

3. Invest in One Statement Outerwear Piece 🧥

This style guide wouldn't be complete without saying: your jacket IS the outfit. A cropped puffer, an oversized fleece, or a waterproof shell in a fun color can carry the entire look. Throw it over literally anything — leggings, a midi skirt, wide-leg pants — and suddenly you're the gorpcore girl everyone's saving inspo from.

4. Accessorize Like a Chic Hiker 🧭

Carabiner keychains clipped to belt loops, technical crossbody bags, sporty sunglasses, a bucket hat — these small details are what separate "I threw this on" from "I STYLED this." Bonus points for a water bottle carrier that doubles as arm candy. No, seriously, they exist and they're iconic.
